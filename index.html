Hello Traveler! Thank you for visiting my portfolio. My name is Juan Carlos, I'm a SoCal Native and primarily a landscape photographer, but I do enjoy taking portraits as well. You could say I'm a "Fullstack Photographer".

I started my photography journey back in 2016 when I noticed all these amazing instagram photos from travelers. I became inspired by the vibes conveyed through their pictures, and I guess you can say the rest is history.

I use a Nikon D3300 and iPhone XR for my pictures. Nothing complex, but I believe there's a lot of beauty in simplicity too! I hope you enjoy your visit. Shoot me a message if you'd like to collab!
